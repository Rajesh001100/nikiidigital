# About NiKii Digital Academy

NiKii Digital Academy (NCA) is a comprehensive educational management platform designed for the **NiKii Computer Academy** in Anthiyur. The platform streamlines the entire student lifecycle, from initial course registration to certification.

## Key Features

### For Students:
- **Online Registration**: Easy-to-use form with course selection, batch preferences, and payment mode selection.
- **Student Dashboard**: A personalized portal (Light Theme) to track attendance, fee payments, and view recent transactions.
- **Resource Center**: Access to course syllabus and study materials uploaded by instructors.
- **Digital Certificates**: Secure digital certificates available for viewing and sharing upon course completion.
- **Automated Notifications**: Instant WhatsApp and Email updates for registration and admission confirmation.

### For Admin & Staff:
- **Registration Management**: View, update, and confirm student admissions.
- **Attendance Tracking**: Digital attendance marking system with automated alerts for absentees.
- **Financial Management**: Record payments, track balances, and generate fee reports (CSV export).
- **CMS (Content Management)**: Manage courses, batch times, and study materials dynamically.
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Visual trends for registrations and revenue.

## Mission
To provide a seamless digital experience for students while empowering academy administrators with data-driven tools to manage educational operations efficiently.
